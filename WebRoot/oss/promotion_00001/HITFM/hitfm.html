What Inspires Us

One of the most fun things about doing what we do is we get to synthesize and try to apply the best from a wide range of thinkers, artists and businesspeople. Here are some of our go-to resources:

  • Nudge
    For showing how little tweaks can reframe how humans process information for much better results.

    More Info

  • ButDoesItFloat.com
    For a cornucopia of design, art, science and philosophical deliciousness.

    More Info

  • Thinking Fast & Slow
    For summarizing the last 30 years of research on judgement and decision making in such an entertaining way.

    More Info

  • Dieter Rams: Ten Principles For Good Design
    Timeless. And how can you argue with a designer named Dieter? Das geht nicht!

    More Info

  • Signal vs Noise
    Because it's still a very well curated set of interesting and smart observations about how people use stuff.

    More Info

  • Baked In
    For making the obvious points that so many corporate leaders have grown to avoid.

    Because it's funny.
